About Colombo

Colombo is the commercial capital of Sri Lanka and a vibrant city that combines modern architecture with colonial buildings and historical landmarks. The city is also known for its delicious cuisine, bustling markets, and scenic coastline.

Top Attractions

  • The National Museum

    The National Museum is a must-visit for anyone interested in the rich history and culture of Sri Lanka. The museum features a wide range of exhibits, including ancient artifacts, royal regalia, and colonial-era memorabilia.

  • Gangaramaya Temple

    Gangaramaya Temple is a fascinating blend of traditional and modern architecture and is home to an impressive collection of Buddhist art and artifacts. Visitors can also explore the temple's peaceful gardens and observe daily rituals and ceremonies.

  • Beira Lake

    Beira Lake is a tranquil oasis in the heart of the city and is a popular spot for picnics, boat rides, and bird watching. Visitors can also explore the nearby Seema Malaka Temple, which is situated on a small island in the middle of the lake.

  • Galle Face Green

    Galle Face Green is a sprawling park and promenade that runs along the coastline of Colombo. It's a popular spot for jogging, kite flying, and enjoying street food from the vendors that line the edge of the park.
